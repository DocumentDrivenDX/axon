### Verdict: REQUEST_CHANGES

### Summary

The plan is roughly 90% converged and the design is clearly implementable, but two spec gaps will force a rework loop rather than a clean execution. First, the "exact" Cargo section omits the new `axon-esf` dependency edge that every re-export and validation refactor in `axon-schema` requires — a straight compile failure. Second, the plan relocates error-message construction into new `RawValidationError`/`SchemaCompileError` types without pinning their `message` to the original `jsonschema` `Display` string, yet the existing substring-matching enhancer and the "prefixes/enhanced errors unchanged" acceptance criteria depend on exactly that string. Pin those two contracts (and audit existing fixtures for incidental format violations) and the plan is ready to implement.
