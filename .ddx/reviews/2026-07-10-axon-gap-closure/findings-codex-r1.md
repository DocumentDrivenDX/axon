### Verdict: BLOCK

### Summary

The plan is not implementation-ready because key contracts still depend on undefined terms: active schema, link declaration scope, transaction atomicity, graph policy order, PostgreSQL qualification, and replica cursor semantics. It also has tracker and baseline risks that can let final readiness close against stale or incomplete evidence. Finish line A is not honestly pilot-ready until those contracts and gates are made testable; finish line B is not complete until FEAT-032 defines consistency, token scope, and policy-change behavior.
