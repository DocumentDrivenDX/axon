### Verdict: REQUEST_CHANGES

### Summary

The plan's central premises hold, but five gaps would still produce incompatible or unsafe outcomes: the registry is incomplete; exemption removal does not close generic entity raw writes; PostgreSQL SERIALIZABLE is asserted but absent; `schema_mismatch` is overloaded; and memory audit is not co-committed. Tracker facts must also be derived from the fetched baseline, and required-link evaluation, payload measurement, replica key/redaction behavior, cursor migration, force-cascade, durability implementation, and ADR drift need explicit treatment.
